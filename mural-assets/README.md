Mural assets for the policy influence timeline

title-card.png
One title card for the board header. Place it at the top of the Mural frame above the lanes.

lane-header-<laneid>.png (6 files: context, research, analysis, policy, outputs, influence)
Row labels for the six lanes. Place one at the start of each horizontal row, left edge of the board, so every sticky in that row lines up under its header.

phase-banner-<phaseid>.png (5 files: origins, sprint, turn, window, landing)
Banners for the five chronological phases. Place across the top of the board, spanning the columns of stickies that fall in each phase's date range, above the month markers.

month-<yyyy-mm>.png (16 files, one per month that appears in the data)
Boxed month markers for the horizontal time spine. Lay them left to right in date order along a single row to form the spine that the lane rows hang beneath.

sticky-<itemid>.png (47 files, one per timeline item)
The individual event stickies. Place each in its lane's row, positioned under the month marker matching its date. A purple star after the title means it was starred on the original wall chart. A yellow highlight behind the title means it was highlighted on the original wall chart.

arrow-<laneid>-solid.png and arrow-<laneid>-dashed.png (12 files, two per lane)
Curved connector arrows, colored per lane, for drawing links between stickies. Use the solid variant for a direct link and the dashed variant for a looser or inferred link. Scale, rotate and flip as needed in Mural to connect specific pairs. See exports/links.csv in the main project folder for the full list of which item connects to which, with labels and style (solid or dashed).

legend-card.png
A key for the board: lane colors, the star convention and the highlight convention. Place in a corner of the board.

All PNGs are rendered at 2x scale for crisp placement at large board sizes. Cards and stickies have transparent backgrounds around them except title-card.png, which is an opaque paper card meant to sit on its own.
